The National Water Model (NWM) is a hydrologic model that can be accessed online through the NOAA websited. We can pull from it in our HTML page to retrieve real-time and future potential streamflow data.
To Learn More: NWM Summary, NWM Article 1, NWM Article 2

Retrieve Reach ID

To pull projections from the NWM, we need to first identify a reach ID. This designates the location that the information will be pulled from. To do so, follow this link: NWM Map.
Find the desired location and zoom in. Colored points designate monitored locations. Click on the point and you'll find the reach ID over the monitored graph.
